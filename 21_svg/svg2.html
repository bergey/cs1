User Input

Buttons

Our programs can respond to buttons the same way as tic-tac-toe or Battleship, and then change an SVG.

Copy one of your animation above that moves only in the X direction and bounces. Add two buttons to move the Y position up and down. What should happen when the Y value is too big or too small to fit in the SVG?

Copy your orbit animation from above. Add two buttons to increase & decrease the radius of the orbit. You will probably need a new variable to keep track of this radius.
